Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport played by two teams, typically of six players each, on a rectangular court divided by a net. The objective is to score points by sending a ball over the net and into the opponentâ€™s court, while preventing them from returning the ball. Teams can hit the ball up to three times before it must be sent over the net. Players use their hands or arms to bump, set, or spike the ball. Volleyball requires teamwork, communication, and athleticism. It is popular worldwide, with major events such as the FIVB Volleyball World Cup and the Olympics showcasing top teams.
