Chain Kho-Kho

Chain Kho-Kho is a variation of the traditional Indian sport Kho-Kho. It is typically played by two teams, with one team chasing and the other running. The main difference in Chain Kho-Kho is that players form a chain by holding hands while running, adding a unique twist to the original format. The chasing team tries to tag members of the running team while maintaining their position in the chain. Speed, coordination, and teamwork are crucial in this sport.
