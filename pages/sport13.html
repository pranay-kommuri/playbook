Table Tennis

Table tennis, also known as ping pong, is a fast-paced indoor sport played on a rectangular table divided by a net. The game is played with lightweight paddles and a small plastic ball. Players or teams take turns hitting the ball over the net, aiming to make it land on the opponent's side of the table in a way that they cannot return it. Points are scored when the opponent fails to return the ball, hits it out of bounds, or makes an illegal serve. Table tennis requires quick reflexes, precision, and agility. It is particularly popular in countries like China, Japan, and Germany.
